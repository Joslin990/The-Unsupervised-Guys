About Us

Think of this channel as the 3 AM gaming session you never want to end.

We started The Unsupervised Guys because we love the chaos of gaming together. Whether we're getting hunted in The Headliners, breaking PEAK with too many mods, or screaming our lungs out in the latest indie horror game, we're doing it for the fun of it.

We don't take ourselves too seriously. We just want to make you laugh, maybe make you jump a little, and share the best moments from our sessions.
